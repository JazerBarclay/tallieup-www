Common questions

How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account from within the app. Go to Settings, scroll to the bottom, and tap Delete Account. This will permanently remove your account and personal data. Circle history that involves other members may be retained for those members' records.
I can't log in to my account
First, check that you're using the correct email address. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot password" option on the login screen — we'll send you a reset link. If you still can't access your account, email us at hello@tallieup.app.
How do I create a circle?
Tap the + button on the Circles tab. Give your circle a name, choose a currency or custom unit, add members, and tap Create. You can add more members later from the circle's settings.
Can I edit an expense after logging it?
Tallie uses an immutable ledger — expenses are permanent once recorded. This keeps the history honest and trustworthy for all group members. If you made a mistake, you can add a correction entry (e.g. a negative expense or adjustment) to balance it out.
How do settlements work?
When someone pays another person back, record it as a Settlement inside the circle. Select who paid and who they paid, enter the amount, and confirm. Balances update instantly for everyone in the circle.
What does closing a circle do?
Closing a circle prevents any new expenses or membership changes. It's useful when a trip or event is finished and you want to lock the records. The full history is preserved and remains readable. You can archive a closed circle to move it out of your active list.
Can I use Tallie with a custom currency (like "beers")?
Yes! When creating a circle, choose Custom unit and name it whatever you like — "beers", "bottles", "tokens", anything. The unit is fixed for the lifetime of the circle so the history always makes sense.
I found a bug — how do I report it?
Please email hello@tallieup.app with a description of what happened, what you expected, and your device and OS version. Screenshots or screen recordings are really helpful.

Tips & tricks

📌 Pin your most-used circles

Long-press a circle or use the pin icon to keep your favourites at the top of the Home screen for quick access.

🔄 Pull to refresh

Pull down on any list screen to refresh the data and see the latest changes from your group.

👥 Add members before adding expenses

Make sure everyone in the group is added to the circle before logging the first expense — it makes splitting much smoother.

🌙 Dark mode support

Tallie respects your system appearance setting. You can also override it in Settings → Appearance.
