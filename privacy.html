Privacy Policy

🚧 Document in progress This Privacy Policy is a placeholder. A full policy compliant with GDPR and App Store requirements will be published before the public launch of Tallie Up. Questions? Contact us.

1. Who we are

Tallie Up ("we", "us", "our") is a mobile application for tracking shared expenses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our App.

2. Data we collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information: email address and display name provided on sign-up.
  • Usage data: circles you create, expenses you log, and members you invite.
  • Preferences: theme, currency, and other in-app settings stored in your profile.

We do not collect payment card information. We do not track your location.

3. How we use your data

  • To provide and maintain the Tallie Up service.
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To allow group members to see shared expense data within circles you participate in.
  • To improve the App based on anonymous usage patterns.

We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Data storage & security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row Level Security enabled. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS. We apply industry-standard security practices and review them regularly.

5. Data sharing

We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Within circles: members of a circle can see the expenses and balances in that circle.
  • Service providers: we use Supabase as our infrastructure provider, subject to their own privacy commitments.
  • Legal requirements: we may disclose data if required by law.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location you may have rights including: access to your data, correction of inaccurate data, deletion of your account and data, and portability of your data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@tallieup.app.

You can delete your account directly within the Tallie Up app, which will remove your personal data from our systems.

7. Cookies

The Tallie Up mobile app does not use cookies. Our website may use minimal, functional cookies to operate correctly. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the service evolves. We will notify you of material changes via the App or by email. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at hello@tallieup.app.
