The problem

Who owes who?

Splitting costs in a group is awkward. Someone always pays upfront, someone always forgets, and nobody can quite remember what was fair.

The group chat method Endless "who got the taxi?" messages that go unanswered for days.
The spreadsheet method One person updates it, nobody else can read it, and it breaks on holiday.
The "I'll get the next one" method Works great until nobody remembers who owed what from six rounds ago.

FAQ

Questions? We have answers.

Do I need an account to use Tallie?
Yes — Tallie uses accounts to keep your data safe, backed up, and synced. Sign up takes under a minute with just your email and a password.
How is Tallie different from splitting apps like Splitwise?
Tallie focuses on clean, isolated circles with an immutable ledger — every entry is permanent, corrections are made as new entries, and there are no silent edits. It also supports custom units (think: beers, tokens, holiday points) beyond standard currencies.
Can I use custom currencies or non-money units?
Yes! Each circle can use any ISO currency (GBP, USD, EUR…) or a completely custom unit — "beers", "festival tokens", "bottles", whatever fits your group. The unit is locked when the circle is created to preserve the meaning of historical records.
What happens when a circle is closed or archived?
Closing a circle prevents any new expenses or membership changes. Archiving makes it fully read-only. Either way, the full history is preserved — you can always look back at what happened.
Is my data safe?
Tallie is powered by Supabase with PostgreSQL and Row Level Security — meaning your data is isolated, encrypted in transit, and only accessible by you. We do not sell your data.
Is there an Android version?
Yes! Tallie is available on both iOS and Android. Download it free from the App Store or Google Play.
